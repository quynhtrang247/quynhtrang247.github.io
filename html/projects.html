Relationship between Corporate Governance and Firm Profitability

Tools

Stata, Excel, Panel Data Econometrics

Project Links

GitHub Report

Overview

Team Research Project | HNX-listed firms | 2010 - 2024

Details

  • Conducted quantitative panel-data analysis to examine how corporate governance mechanisms affect firm profitability.
  • Cleaned and structured panel data in Stata, including type conversion, variable construction, log transformation, and winsorization.
  • Applied model diagnostics including VIF, Hausman, heteroskedasticity, autocorrelation, and time fixed-effects tests.
  • Estimated Fixed Effects regressions with clustered robust standard errors and interpreted empirical results for the final report.

Impact of News Sentiment on Stock Returns: Evidence from VN30

Tools

Python, Jupyter Notebook, Panel Data Analysis, Web Scraping

Project Links

GitHub Report

Overview

Team Research Project | VN30 Index | Jan 2023 - Jun 2024

Details

  • Developed a Python workflow in Jupyter Notebook to collect financial news, retrieve VN30 market data, and construct a panel dataset for analysis.
  • Processed and merged firm-level sentiment, macro news, stock prices, and trading volume data for econometric modeling.
  • Applied sentiment scoring and estimated Pooled OLS, Fixed Effects, and Random Effects models with diagnostic tests.
  • Visualized and interpreted the results, finding a positive and statistically significant relationship between firm-specific sentiment and short-term stock returns.
